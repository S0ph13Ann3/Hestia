H E S T I A




O u r M i s s i o n

Our mission is to provide a platform for all small and local business to share their stories and struggles throughout the COVID-19 pandemic. Our local businesses, although small, are the very core of communities we live in. We believe that giving back to our communities is the best way to heal. We showcase alternatives to big corporate chains that often use brands that are bad for our enviornment and community.





Why H E S T I A ?

We support our local and small business in our community and allow them to promote themselves on our platform. We hold live Q & A questions on our Instagram account with these businesses to share to a larger audience on social media about their business and why it's a better alternative. By encouraging users to explore their local shops, not only do we support local businesses, we discourage the growth of fast fashion industries, and we are also reducing the amount of cross-community spread.






Future Plans

In future iterations, we hope to create a mobile app of our website so that users can have an eaiser access to our page. Additionally, we will also create a search function on our website that uses the user locations to find local and small business near them. Local businesses will also be able to upload photos of their products and deals too. We also plan on hosting our own podcast, interviewing local business owners, workers, and more. Hopefully, with enough support, our platform will be able to grow and continue to bring attention to our local businesses.
